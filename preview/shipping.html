Shipping & Delivery

Fast, reliable shipping on all orders

Free Shipping

Enjoy FREE Next Day Delivery on all UK orders. Free international shipping on all orders worldwide.

UK Shipping

  • Next Day Delivery: FREE on all orders
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days - FREE

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide with FREE international shipping:

  • Europe: 3-7 business days - FREE
  • USA/Canada: 5-10 business days - FREE
  • Australia/NZ: 7-14 business days - FREE
  • Rest of World: 7-21 business days - FREE

Please note: International customers may be responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees.

Order Processing

Orders placed before 2:00 PM GMT on business days are typically processed and shipped the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM GMT or on weekends/holidays will be processed the next business day.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account or contacting our support team.

Shipping Restrictions

Some products may have shipping restrictions to certain states or countries due to local regulations. If your order cannot be shipped to your location, we will notify you and issue a full refund.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your order.

Questions?

Contact our support team at support@maxlabs.com or call 1-800-MAX-LABS for shipping inquiries.
